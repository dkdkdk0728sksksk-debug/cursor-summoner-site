Privacy Policy for Cursor Summoner

Effective Date: 2026-06-30

Cursor Summoner does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

Data Collection

Cursor Summoner does not collect personal data.

The app does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • User-generated content

Tracking

Cursor Summoner does not track users.

The app does not use advertising identifiers, tracking technologies, third-party analytics SDKs, or advertising SDKs.

Network Access

Cursor Summoner does not require network access for its current local visual effect features.

The app does not upload user data to any server.

Local Settings

Cursor Summoner stores app preferences locally on the user's Mac.

These preferences may include:

  • Hotkey settings
  • Magic Circle effect size settings
  • Random size setting
  • Reduce Motion setting
  • Launch at Login setting
  • Optional Pointer Recall setting

These settings remain on the user's device and are not transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Permissions

Cursor Summoner does not require:

  • Accessibility permission
  • Input Monitoring permission
  • Screen Recording permission

Children's Privacy

Cursor Summoner does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change.
